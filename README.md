## 🔬 About Us

We are a dedicated community of technologists, researchers, and innovators at **VIT Bhopal** who are deeply interested in the future of biology and computing. The **NeuroByte Society** focuses on building a strong, interdisciplinary student community around **Bioinformatics, Biotechnology, AI, and Computational Biology**. 

Our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and hands-on learning through workshops, hackathons, research publications, and open-source technical projects.
